## üìÇ Your Medical Card & Documents

The bot acts as your personal health assistant.

Tap **üìÑ Medical Card** to open your private **Live Dashboard** directly inside Telegram. It includes:

You can store your medical history securely in the bot.

1. Tap **üì§ Upload Documents** to see instructions.

> **Supported**: Photos (.jpg), Documents (.pdf, .dcm), Videos (.mp4), Voice Messages (.ogg).
